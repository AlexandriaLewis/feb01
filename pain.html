IT HURTS

The #1 most common question.

Yea sure it hurts, but pain is relative. Whether it's from cooking or exercise you should know your own pain thresh hold. Asking a stranger if a tattoo hurt is like asking wait-staff what their favorite menu item is. Honestly you don't know them and their tastes(pain threshhold) is different from yours.
